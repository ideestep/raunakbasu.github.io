Welcome to the Basu Neuro Lab

We study the neural mechanisms underlying navigation and decision-making, with a focus on how the brain builds and uses internal maps of the world.

Our work combines systems neuroscience, behavior, and computational approaches to understand flexible cognition in complex environments.
